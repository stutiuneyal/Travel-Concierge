Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival in Dubai

Morning: Arrive in Dubai at 12:05 PM. Check into your hotel and relax after your flight.

Afternoon: Take a leisurely stroll around the hotel area or visit a nearby café for lunch.

Evening: Explore the Dubai Mall and enjoy dinner at one of the restaurants with views of the Burj Khalifa.

Notes: Keep the first day light to adjust to the new time zone. If it rains, consider indoor activities at the mall.

Day 2 - Burj Khalifa & Dubai Aquarium

Morning: Visit Burj Khalifa early to avoid crowds. Pre-book tickets for the observation deck.

Afternoon: Explore the Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo located in the Dubai Mall.

Evening: Dinner at a restaurant in the Dubai Mall, followed by a visit to Burj Park for stunning views of the Burj Khalifa at night.

Notes: Check weather forecasts; if rain is expected, plan indoor activities.

Day 3 - Desert Safari Adventure

Morning: Relax in the morning. Prepare for the Desert Safari.

Afternoon: Join a Desert Safari tour that includes dune bashing, camel rides, and sandboarding.

Evening: Enjoy a BBQ dinner and cultural performances at the desert camp.

Notes: Ensure to stay hydrated during the safari. Dress comfortably for outdoor activities.

Day 4 - Cultural Exploration

Morning: Visit the Dubai Museum to learn about the city's history.

Afternoon: Explore the Al Fahidi Historical Neighborhood and enjoy lunch at a local café.

Evening: Visit The Museum of the Future for an interactive experience.

Notes: If rain is forecasted, consider indoor museums or cultural centers.

Day 5 - Day Trip to Abu Dhabi

Morning: Take a guided tour to Abu Dhabi. Visit the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.

Afternoon: Explore the Louvre Abu Dhabi and enjoy lunch nearby.

Evening: Return to Dubai and have dinner at a local restaurant.

Notes: Book the Abu Dhabi tour in advance to secure a spot.

Day 6 - Beach Day & Shopping

Morning: Relax at Jumeirah Beach. Enjoy the sun and sea.

Afternoon: Visit the Mall of the Emirates for shopping and indoor skiing if interested.

Evening: Dinner at a restaurant in the Mall of the Emirates.

Notes: If rain is expected, consider indoor activities at the mall.

Day 7 - Departure

Morning: Last-minute shopping or sightseeing near the hotel.

Afternoon: Check out of the hotel and head to the airport.

Evening: Flight back to Bangalore.

Notes: Ensure to leave for the airport with ample time for check-in.
